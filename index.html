My Unsolicited Thoughts on Each Letter of the Alphabet

Welcome to my webpage. I pondered quite a bit on what this was going to be, perhaps a resume, maybe a collection of relevant data, but then I had a moment of clarity. No one knows my personal opinions about the letters of the alphabet, and that's a travesty. This website rights that wrong.

You'll find plenty to explore on here. From A, to B, to C, to, well, you get the idea. Though the writing is at times informal, the gravity of my thoughts are immense. I don't deal in frivolity, and that'll become clear. The clipart I used is the best of the best of the first two lines of google image search.

When you're done clicking through my thoughts, I'd like you to give my page a cool facebook like at the bottom. Engagement is what keeps me going, without it, I disappear into the void. The void is liminal and therefore not necessarily a good or bad place to be, but it's dark, and I'm afraid of the dark, so please engage. Thanks :)
